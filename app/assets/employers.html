Product Tour

MyDoc consultation platform

MyDoc connects healthcare professionals and patients via a secure messaging and video consultation platform. This allows the remote provision of routine care and follow ups.

MyDoc ensures care is given in the correct context as providers can see the employee's secure patient health record and previous communication history.

Medical Concierge

People shouldn’t be expected to always know the level of care they need. Our concierge service can provide trained medical care at the touch of a button. This guarantees timely intervention and great service whilst ensuring appropriate encounters are handled in a low-cost setting by the right healthcare professional for the job.

Actionable Employee Health Data

PDPA and HIPAA compliant usage reports and aggregate health analytics allow you to get insight into the health risks facing your employees to better target preventative programs and plan the provision of more cost effective services in real time.

Patient Engagement

Patients can store and share text, video, pictures, lab results and prescriptions with their healthcare providers. This can facilitate remote monitoring, e-referrals to specialists or delivery of care programmes between visits.

MyDoc revolutionising health screenings

In one recent example, a routine employer-promoted health screening using MyDoc resulted in a 4x increase in the number of employees following up on their basic results. Greater follow ups mean greater preventative care.
Employees get the follow up care they need and you get the data necessary to plan future health and wellness provision.
